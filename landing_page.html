ABOUT US

Founded in 1940 by Howard Stark, Stark Industries is a multinational industrial company and the largest tech conglomerate in the world. Stark Industries engages in the research, design, development, manufacturing, integration, and sustainment of advanced technology systems, products, and services.

OUR VISION

Be the global leader in supporting our customers' missions, strengthening security, and advancing scientific discovery. We will create a better future for everyone.

MEET THE FACES OF STARK INDUSTRIES

TONY STARK

OWNER & CHAIRMAN

Former CEO of Stark Industries, Tony Stark is the prodigy of founder Howard Stark. At age 15, he graduated MIT with master's degrees in engineering and physics. When he's not saving the world as Iron Man, Stark spends his time developing inventions that expand the limits of possibility.

PEPPER POTTS

CHEIF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Pepper Potts received her MBA at Stamford University and has worked at Stark Industries for over 10 years. Under her leadership, the company's net worth has recently doubled. Potts has received multiple awards for her leadership skills, including the prestigious Business Role Model of the Year.
